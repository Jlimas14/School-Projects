Welcome to ZooLand

Meet the newest and most inclusive zoo in the world, ZooLand

From lions and meerkats, to dinosaurs and dogs, we have created protective and safe habitats for all animals that come into our zoo.


As a community fund project we plan to grow even larger including all kinds of exotic animals that you've never seen and common animals that we all know and love
